Team

Poptop is a small business started by collectors for collectors. Here at Poptop we know how to provide our customers with the best service. We have over five years of online retail experience and on top of that we are collectors ourselves. We started selling Pops on Amazon, eBay, and other major online marketplaces about a year ago. As collectors we didn't like the prices some items had to be sold at in order to cover fees and other expenses on the major marketplaces. We decided to start our own website to provide other collectors better more competitive prices than we previously could.
